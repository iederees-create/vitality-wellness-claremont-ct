If you purchased this template, you must replace every piece of demo business and treatment information — including contact details, pricing, team profiles and testimonials — with your own accurate, truthful content in src/config/siteConfig.ts before publishing your site.

If your business offers services that are professionally regulated in your region (for example treatments requiring a licensed practitioner, medical aesthetics, or injectable treatments), you are responsible for ensuring your published site accurately reflects your real qualifications, licensing, insurance and any legally required disclosures for your jurisdiction. This template does not include, and should not be read as providing, that compliance information.

Treatment suitability should always be assessed by a qualified professional — not by this website or its Consultation Finder tool.
